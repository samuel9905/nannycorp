About NannyCorp

At NANNYCORP, we believe every family deserves a caring and well-behaved Nanny. Whether you’re looking to find the perfect nanny or train your current nanny, we’ve got your back. Nannies play an important role within your family environment.

As a nanny agency, NANNYCORP is committed to helping families in find the right nanny for their home. We cater for busy working parents and families. Families who are on the go and need a domestic nanny to help them manage their households and busy schedules.

Finding the right nanny is vital to managing a family home in a happier and organised environment. We know how difficult it can be to have a new nanny in the home. We also know when you find that right person, what a difference it makes to your family life.

At NannyCorp, we don't do regular nannies, we provide trainings to our nannies in order to help them take better care of your wards.
Our Nannies go through child behavioral and psychology training, security training, we equip them with first aid skills and child tantrum management skills.

Our nannies are educated and well equipped with the latest trends in technology, as such they are able to help your children with some of their assignments, play educational videos for them and ensure they are kept clean at all times.
